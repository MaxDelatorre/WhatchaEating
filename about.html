About Our Calorie Calculator, Whatcha Eating

Welcome to Watcha Eating!

We believe that understanding your nutritional needs is key to achieving your health and fitness goals. Our intuitive calorie calculator is designed to help you track your daily caloric intake, set personalized goals, and make informed choices about your diet.

Who We Are

We are a team Computer Science students from CalState Fullerton that are passionate about empowering individuals on their wellness journeys. We understand that every body is unique, and our mission is to provide you with the tools you need to succeed—whether you're looking to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain a healthy lifestyle.

What We Offer

Our calorie calculator provides:

  • Personalized Calorie Estimates: We deliver tailored calorie calculations that fit your lifestyle.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Our easy-to-navigate platform allows you to log your meals and track your progress effortlessly, ensuring a seamless experience.

Why Choose Us?

  • Accuracy: Our calculations are based on the U.S Department of Agriculture's (USDA) calorie database to ensure you receive the most accurate information possible.

  • Community Support: Join a community of like-minded individuals who are on their own health journeys. Support Computer Science students like us on our web development journey.

  • Open Source: Our website is fully open source and open to changes. As a University Project, we aim to take feedback and learn along the way

Join Us on Your Journey

Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your approach, Watcha Eating is here to support you every step of the way. Take control of your nutrition, track your progress, and unlock a healthier, happier you.
